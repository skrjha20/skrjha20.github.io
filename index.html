About me

Hello, my name is Natassha Selvaraj. I am currently pursuing a computer science degree, and I major in data science.

During my free time, I like to write and code. Writing has been a childhood passion, I loved it so much I even wrote a book when I was 9 years old!

My interest in coding came later, when I realized I enjoyed problem solving, and creating programs to help perform small everyday tasks seemed like magic to me.

As a data science major, I started to learn how to use statistics and programming to derive insights and analyze data, which sparked my interest as well. After taking a couple of online classes and reading a few books, I started working on side projects, and on solving data problems I found interesting.

I enjoy coding, and love to write. So during my free time, I write about what I code! I tell stories around the data science projects I create, and like to share my insights through storytelling.

I publish some of my work on Medium publications, and will showcase them here. I currently am a writer in Medium publications such as The Startup, Towards Data Science, Better Programming, Level Up Coding, and Data Driven Investor.
